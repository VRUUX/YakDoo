Got questions about dog chews?

Do Yak-milk based edible dog chews have any dental health benefit?

Yes, A study published in the Journal of Veterinary Dentistry found that regular use of dental chews can reduce plaque by up to 69%.

Dogs need proper dental care too. Ignoring it can lead to bad breath, plaque buildup, gum disease, and even serious health complications later on.

A chewing activity along with the ingredients of the edible chew helps break down the plaque and prevents plaque build up.

Are YakDoo dog chews are nutritious? 

YakDoo Dog Chews are edible and made from Indian Himalayan Yak's milk. These chews are high in protein.

My Dog is lactose intolerant, can I feed YakDoo dog chews?

We recommend to consult your pet's vet before feeding any edible chews. YakDoo dog chews are produced from Yak's milk so consulting vet is highly recommended for lactose intolerant dogs.

How long can I keep YakDoo dog chews stored?

Typically YakDoo dog chews have longer shelf life and can be stored even up to 18-24 months without much of a specialized storage requirements.

Do YakDoo dog chews saves me a charges for my dog's dental health?

No. While YakDoo Dog Chews are naturally strong and highly nutritious, they are not a replacement for breed-specific regular dental care. For optimal dental health for your pet, both YakDoo chews and professional veterinary dental care are essential and work in combination.
